## üë• For Church Members

### How to Register (First Time Only)

1. **Visit the Website**
   - Go to: https://attedance-web-app.onrender.com/
   - Click **"Register as Member"**

2. **Fill Out Registration Form**
   - Enter your full name
   - Enter your phone number (important!)
   - Create a username and password
   - Provide additional information (optional but helpful):
     - Date of birth
     - Gender
     - Email address

3. **Submit & Confirm**
   - Click **"Sign Up"**
   - You're now registered! ‚úÖ

---

### How to Mark Attendance

1. **Go to Attendance Page**
   - Visit: https://attedance-web-app.onrender.com/
   - Or click **"Mark Attendance"** from menu

2. **Select Your Service**
   - Choose the service you're attending (e.g., "Sunday Service", "Bible Study")

3. **Enter Service Code**
   - Look for the code displayed at the church venue
   - Code is typically shown on screen/banner
   - Example: "SUN2025" or "WED123"

4. **Search for Your Name**
   - Start typing your name in the search box
   - Click your name when it appears

5. **Submit**
   - Click **"Mark Attendance"**
   - You'll see a confirmation message ‚úÖ

**Done!** Your attendance has been recorded.

### Frequently Asked Questions (Members)

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Contact your church administrator to reset it.

**Q: What if I don't have a smartphone?**
A: Visit the attendance desk at church - volunteers will help you check in.

**Q: The service code isn't working. Why?**
A: Make sure you're selecting the correct service and entering the code exactly as shown. Codes are case-sensitive.

**Q: Can I mark attendance for my family?**
A: Each family member should register separately and mark their own attendance.

**Q: What if I forget to mark attendance?**
A: Contact your church administrator after the service - they can manually add you.

### How to Create a New Service

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Click **"Settings"** in sidebar
   - Find **"Church Services"** section

2. **Add Service**
   - Click **"Add New Service"**
   - Enter service name (e.g., "Sunday Worship", "Wednesday Bible Study")
   - Generate a unique code (e.g., "SUN2025")
   - Click **"Save"**

3. **Display Code at Venue**
   - Show the code on screen/banner during service
   - Members will use this code to mark attendance

---

### How to Use Offline Mode (No Internet!)

**Perfect for churches with poor internet connection**

#### Preparation (At Home, While Online):
1. **Open Offline Mode**
   - Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Click **"Offline Mode"**
   - Page will load all member data

2. **Keep Browser Open**
   - Don't close the tab/browser
   - Take your device to church

#### At Church (No Internet Needed):
1. **Select Service**
   - Choose today's service from dropdown

2. **Search Members**
   - Type member name or phone number
   - Click on member to select

3. **Mark Present**
   - Click **"Mark Present"**
   - Member is saved locally on your device

4. **Repeat**
   - Continue marking all attendees

#### After Service (Back Online):
1. **Click "Sync Now"**
   - All offline attendance uploads to server
   - You'll see confirmation: "15 records synced ‚úÖ"

**Pro Tip:** Always pre-load the offline page before leaving for church!

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Website Won't Load
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Wait 30 seconds (Render may be waking up)

### Can't Login
- Verify username/password
- Check CAPS LOCK is off
- Contact administrator to reset password

### Attendance Not Showing
- Wait 1-2 minutes for sync (if using offline mode)
- Refresh the page
- Check if you selected correct service

### Offline Mode Not Working
- Ensure you loaded the page while online first
- Don't close browser tab
- Check browser local storage isn't cleared
